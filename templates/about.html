About Us

     QWALLITY is a start-up IT company focused on the software quality assurance. The company was established in 2021. The idea of starting the company is based on the various gaps discovered in the field of software quality assurance over the years, such as

    • shortage of qualified specialists

    • market demand for various testing types

    • lack of practical education

    • concentration of specialists in Yerevan

    • low-level software security

  Our Mission The company emphasizes the importance of the software quality and software security. Our main goal is…

     - To develop the QA field in all regions of Armenia, to train QA specialists ready to work in different regions

     - To give practical education in main testing types

     - To Increase the level of software security

   Our Services QWALLITY has 4 directions…

     • QA Trainings

     • QA Outsourcing

     • QA Consulting

     • Professional Services
